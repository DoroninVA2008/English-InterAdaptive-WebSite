Grammar Reference

Quick overview of English tenses

English Tenses — Quick Reference Table
Tense Structure Example Use Case
Present Simple S + V(s/es) She works every day. Habits, facts
Present Continuous S + am/is/are + V-ing He is reading now. Actions happening now
Past Simple S + V(ed/irreg.) They went to school. Completed past actions
Future Simple S + will + V I will call you. Predictions, decisions
Present Perfect S + have/has + V3 We have finished. Past action, present result
